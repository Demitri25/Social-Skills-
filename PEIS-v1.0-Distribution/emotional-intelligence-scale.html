Pediatric Emotional Intelligence Scale (PEIS)

Ages 2.5 - 18 Years | 30-Item Assessment

Assessment Information

Instructions

Rate each item based on how well it describes the child's typical behavior. Use the following scale:

  • 0 = Never - This behavior is never observed
  • 1 = Rarely - This behavior occurs less than 25% of the time
  • 2 = Sometimes - This behavior occurs 25-50% of the time
  • 3 = Often - This behavior occurs 50-75% of the time
  • 4 = Always - This behavior occurs more than 75% of the time
