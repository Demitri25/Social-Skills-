TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS AND VALIDATION

The Pediatric Emotional Intelligence Scale (PEIS) has been developed using established psychometric principles and validated through comprehensive research studies. The assessment tool provides:

- Standardized administration procedures
- Age-appropriate norms for children 2.5-18 years
- Reliable and valid measurement across five EI domains
- Professional-grade scoring algorithms
- Comprehensive interpretive reports

For complete technical documentation, validation studies, and research bibliography, please refer to the Technical Manual included with this distribution.
